About Garments

With over two decades of experience in the garment industry, Garments has established itself as a leading manufacturer of high-quality clothing. We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern technology to deliver exceptional products.

Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with advanced machinery for both knit and woven fabric production, operated by skilled professionals who specialize in various fabric constructions and garment manufacturing techniques.

We excel in knit fabric production including single jersey, double jersey, rib, interlock, and fleece, as well as woven fabric manufacturing using cotton, polyester, blends, and specialty fibers with advanced dyeing and finishing capabilities.

We pride ourselves on our commitment to sustainability, ethical manufacturing practices, and building long-term partnerships with our clients worldwide.
